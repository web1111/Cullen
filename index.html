Welcome to Cullen Community & Residential Centre

Many thanks for stopping by

Our main objective is to keep our public facilites open and available for the benefit of our community and visitors. Otherwise faced with closure, despite public demand, a plucky team of local volunteers have decided to take over the management and running of the centre.

As a registered charity, our primary mission is to develop a sustainable income to meet the ongoing running costs of the centre, and to re-invest to protect and further develop our community services as far as possible.

We would of course welcome as much support as you can provide, though please just enjoy the use of OUR facilities to the maximum!
