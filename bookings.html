Want to hire a room or make a residential booking?

How to place a Booking

We're currently reviewing various tools to find the optimum online booking system to make it easier to enable availability checking, booking and handling payments from within this site. We hope to have this in place soon. Thank you for your patience.

Placing residential bookings or hiring rooms still needs to be done manually, as per the general procedure below.

  • Review our Facilities Page and Contact to Check Availability
    email: bookings@cullencrc.org.uk
    phone: (+44) 1542 840701
  • Supply Your Details
  • Supply Billing Info & Deposit Where Required
  • Receive Booking Confirmation
  • Supply Final Payment upon Delivery
