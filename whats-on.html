What's happening in the community

Keeping you updated on activities, news, events and local projects

We're currently reviewing various online tools to enable members of the community to sign up to connect, collaborate and keep informed of our latest developments. In the meantime, please refer to our Centre's own facebook page for a running commentary.

Within Cullen there are several other active online social groups, some of which are listed below, that can help provide more specific news, events and goings on around Cullen within each of their own areas of concern.

Cullen & Deskford Community Council

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/1470486263217326/

This group is open to anyone who resides and or votes in Cullen or Deskford that wishes to contact the council about any matters.

Cullen Amenities

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/682299755461691/

Following the controversial decision by the local authorities to close Cullen's public toilets in the town square and at the beach, a group of volunteers is trying to raise funds and organise to re-open these vital public faciliites. This group has been set up so that members of the Cullen Amenities group can discuss plans and organise work to be carried out on Cullen's public toilets.

Cullen Coop Events Calendar

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/118933125475895/

Keeping you informed of events in and around Cullen

Cullen Chat

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/cullenchat

A place for general chat about Cullen (Scotland), to buy & sell items, and to find information on local businesses and events taking place in the area. Professional traders in the local area, please keep your for sale posts to once a week and group the photos together. Local Cullen shops etc, post whenever you want - it is a Cullen site!

Cullen Past & Present

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/373284116022602/

A social media site sharing photos and memories of Cullen.

Cullen Sea School Rowers

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/1182889651760103/

A place to keep up to date on rowing activities in Cullen associated with the Cullen Sea School

Cullen Community Allotment Association

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/475723762447427/

This is our fun page for boasting and bragging about our plots. Swapping ideas, plants, photos, seeds, recipes, message events, slug control ideas and more.

Cullen Past & Present Volunteer Group

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/290537881283705/

Volunteer projects in Cullen, helping to make things more accessible and trying to make improvements for everyone's benefit

Cullen Links Junior Golf

  • https://www.facebook.com/groups/1397042010549683/

This group will keep all aspects of Junior golf up to date regarding Cullen Links Golf Club. This includes Clubgolf coaching for beginners, Roving Pro and Junior Competitions.
