Activities available at the centre

How to take part in current activities - or organise a new activity!

We have many groups using our facilities as a venue for club type activities as well as different types of community events.

To take part in any currently active centre activities:

  • Check the Activity Calendar below
  • Get in Touch to Express an Interest & Supply Your Contact Details
    email: activities@cullencrc.org.uk
    phone: (+44) 1542 840701
  • Activity Leader Will Separately Respond with Availability & Joining Instructions
  • Arrange any Activity Payments Directly with Activity Leader

Can't see anything on the calendar that takes your fancy? - We would welcome your feedback on any activities you would be keen to participate in. Perhaps we can help source supply to meet demand, and to help us evolve to better meet the needs of the community.

To organise a new activity yourself and hire a venue for it for your group, please refer to our Bookings page.
